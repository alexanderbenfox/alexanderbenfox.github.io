Space Game

This is/was my first project - it's currently on indefinite hiatus.

The goal for this game was to make a side scroller with high difficulty, but lots of movement options and combos - sort of like a fighting game.

I was heavily inspired by Bloodborne for this one because I had just begun development when Bloodborne came out - so I decided to make it a "Metroidvania set in the wild west".

The sprite-work and audio design became a little too much for me so I halted development during school finals and never came back - I may continue working if I got some help. You can check out the umoga page here.
