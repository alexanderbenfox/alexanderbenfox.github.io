Warp Hunter

Play as Arch, bred to serve the empire, she has turned rogue to uncover the truth and right the wrongs of her past.

With only the memory of her service to the galactic empire in tact, Arch awakens deep in the catacombs of an alien life form. To make your way out and regain your memories, you must fight through this alient planet and build a communication device, but the life forms of this new planet don't seem to like outsiders.

    Game Features:
  • Collect powerful upgrades to your blaster and blade
  • Metroidvania style worlds - each section is connected to the other parts
  • Full pixel art graphics
  • Hook shot and grapple mechanics
  • Mechanized robot gameplay sections

This game has currently halted development

I did the least amount of work on this game, but it was a place for me to design a good hierarchical program structure that was not present in EHB: creating a game objects class that is the parent for all other classes in the game. Tiled maps contained tile objects that could be extended to special tile classes, etc.

Major inspirations for this game include:

  • Super Metroid
  • Death's Gambit
  • Dead Space
