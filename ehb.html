Edward Headbedwardly/ Project Torchman

Edward Headbedwardly was a sleepy cowboy thrown into a terrible nightmare! You are just a simple doctor, called to a strange town to investigate a peculiar plague that haunts the citizens... As you go further into the town's strange surroundings: underground libraries, reminants of cultist rituals in the clock tower, and ruins of a forgotten civilization deep in the desert, you realize that there's more to this town, and this plague, that meets the eye.

By utilizing your magical candle and trusty pistol, you can bring light into this dark world. Given to you by an eerie fortune teller, your candle can transform it's flame into any weapon that you can find. The cards created during dark rituals give the flame power.
