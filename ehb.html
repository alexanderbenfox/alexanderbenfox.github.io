Edward Headbedwardly/ Project Torchman

A sleepy cowboy is thrown into a terrible nightmare! You are just a simple doctor called investigate a peculiar plague that haunts the citizens of a strange town by the name of Animas. As you go further into the town's strange surroundings you find underground libraries, reminants of cultist rituals in the clock tower, and ruins of a forgotten civilization deep in the desert.

By utilizing your magical candle and trusty pistol, you can bring light into this dark world. Given to you by an eerie fortune teller, your candle can transform it's flame into any weapon that you can find. The cards created during dark rituals give the flame power. Corrupted and malevolent souls are trying to keep the secrets of Animas hidden and are not fond of outsiders...

    Game Features:
  • Card combo system that allows you to customize your play experience. Attack with a powerful Greatsword and then get a quick dagger stab to finish off your enemies.
  • Metroidvania style worlds - each section is connected to the other parts
  • Full pixel art graphics
  • Quick and precise combat system that requires you to utilize all of your tools: bombs, lassos, pistols
  • Horse riding gameplay
